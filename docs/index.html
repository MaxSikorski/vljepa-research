How SALT Works

Two-stage training that eliminates EMA — the most complex part of JEPA training.

Stage 1 V-Pixel Teacher Training No EMA

Train a ViT encoder with pixel reconstruction (MAE-style). The decoder is discarded after training.

Stage 2 Frozen-Teacher JEPA No EMA

Freeze the Stage 1 teacher. Train a student + predictor to predict the teacher's latent representations.

Key insight: "Weak teacher, strong student" — a small teacher can train a larger student. Student loss predicts downstream accuracy (R² = 0.951).
